Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

The business registration process typically takes 5-10 working days, depending on the type of entity and the completeness of your documentation. With our expertise, we work to make this process as efficient as possible.

For company registration, you'll need identification documents of directors/shareholders, proof of address, memorandum and articles of association, and details of shareholding structure. We'll guide you through collecting all necessary documents during our initial consultation.

NGO registration costs vary depending on the type and scope of the organization. Our fees include both government fees and our service charges. We provide a detailed breakdown of all costs during our initial consultation for full transparency.

No, you don't need to be physically present in Uganda. We can handle the entire registration process on your behalf with the proper authorization. However, some documents may require your signature, which can be arranged remotely.

Our post-registration services include annual returns filing, tax compliance, regulatory updates, license renewals, and other compliance-related services to ensure your business or organization maintains good standing with Ugandan authorities.
